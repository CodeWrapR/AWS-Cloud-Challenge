💡 Study Tips & Resources

📚 Study Schedule
Dedicate 30-45 minutes daily for consistent learning. Break complex topics into smaller, manageable sessions for better retention.
🎯 Hands-On Practice
Create a free AWS account and practice with the services you learn. Hands-on experience reinforces theoretical knowledge.
🔄 Spaced Repetition
Review flashcards regularly using spaced repetition. Focus more time on topics you find challenging.
👥 Community Learning
Join AWS study groups and forums. Discussing concepts with others helps deepen your understanding and fills knowledge gaps.
